The latent space dimension needs to be known beforehand becasuse the transformer needs to be initialized with it.

First pretrain models. Start with the transformer:
pretrain_se.py

and then pretrain the LSTM, Matrix, BoT and Graph models that produce the guidance embeddings:
train_all_latent.sh

(This step was below the next step but made more sense here)
Then make the contrastive dataset bundle, to facilitate LaCtA training :
make_contrastive_bundled_dataset.py

Then train contrastive space:
train_all_contrastive.sh

Then train LaCtA:
train_all_lacta.sh
